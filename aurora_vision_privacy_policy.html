Privacy Policy for Aurora Vision

Last updated: November 13, 2025

Introduction

Aurora Vision ('we', 'our', or 'us') is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Aurora Vision (the 'App').

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

Aurora Vision uses your device's GPS location to provide personalized Northern Lights forecasts and recommendations:

  • Real-time location: Used to find the best viewing spots near you
  • Background location (optional): If you enable notifications, we monitor aurora conditions in your area (within 25km radius)
  • Location permissions: You can revoke location access at any time through your device settings

Your location data is processed locally on your device and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

2. Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the App:

  • App features used (which screens you visit)
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Device information (OS version, device model)
  • App performance metrics

3. User Preferences

We store your settings locally on your device:

  • Language preference (Icelandic/English)
  • Notification settings and KP threshold
  • Premium subscription status (if applicable)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide aurora forecasts: Calculate visibility scores based on your location
  • Location recommendations: Suggest the best spots to view Northern Lights
  • Smart notifications: Alert you when aurora conditions are favorable in your area
  • App improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Customer support: Respond to your inquiries and technical issues

Third-Party Data Sources

Aurora Vision retrieves weather and aurora data from the following trusted sources:

  • NOAA: Ovation aurora model, KP index, geomagnetic forecasts
  • Veðurstofa Íslands: Cloud cover from weather stations
  • Open-Meteo: Backup weather data

We do not share your personal data with these services. The App simply retrieves publicly available forecast data.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

Aurora Vision displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data.

You can opt out by: Settings Google Ads Opt out of Ads Personalization, or upgrade to Premium.

Google Firebase

We use Firebase for analytics and crash reporting. Firebase may collect anonymous usage analytics, crash logs, and device information.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage: All preferences stored locally on your device
  • No cloud storage: We do not store your personal data on remote servers
  • Encryption: Data transmitted uses HTTPS encryption

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data the App stores
  • Delete: Clear all local data by uninstalling the App
  • Revoke permissions: Disable location or notifications in device settings
  • Opt out of ads: Disable personalized ads or upgrade to Premium

Children's Privacy

Aurora Vision does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Check the 'Last updated' date above.
