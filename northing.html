Northing Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

Introduction

Northing ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Northing does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Location Services

  • Your device location is accessed locally to provide compass and navigation features
  • Location data never leaves your device
  • No location history is stored or transmitted

Device Sensors

  • Compass and magnetometer data is used only for real-time navigation
  • Sensor data is not logged, stored, or transmitted

Offline Functionality

Northing works completely offline using pre-loaded geographic data. No internet connection is required after installation.

Third-Party Services

Northing does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security concerns related to personal information.

Children's Privacy

Northing is safe for users of all ages as it collects no personal information.

User Preferences

The app stores only your acceptance of Terms of Use locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This information never leaves your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
